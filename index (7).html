About Us

We're a Small Local Team Who Cares

We're a small, local team right here in the Rochester, NY area — and we started this because we genuinely care about where food comes from and who grows it.

Small farms in Upstate New York are doing incredible work, but getting their products directly into the hands of local families isn't always easy. We wanted to fix that — not by building another big platform, but by creating something personal, simple, and community-rooted.

Our goal is straightforward: support small local farms and add the convenience of delivery so you don't need to make another trip. Fresh eggs, sourced nearby, brought right to your door — no detours, no middleman, no hassle.
